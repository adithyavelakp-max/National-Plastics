About Us

Founded by Mr. Kandasamy, our company has been a leader in the PP film industry in the southern part of India since 1993. With over 30 years of industry experience, we specialize in the manufacturing and wholesale supply of extruded blown film polypropylene tubes and bags, with an impressive production capacity of 1,000 tonnes per year.

Our mission is to provide high-grade virgin polypropylene bags while ensuring fast and reliable service. We are dedicated to delivering an exceptional packaging experience for our customers, meeting their needs with quality and efficiency.
